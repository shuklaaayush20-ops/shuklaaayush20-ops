# Stay Safe Online: Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

### General Digital Safety
1. **Use strong, unique passwords:** Avoid using your birthdate or phone number. Use long passphrases and enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all accounts.
2. **Be skeptical of "urgent" requests:** Scammers create artificial urgency. Always take a minute to verify before sharing information or clicking links.
3. **Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks:** Never make financial transactions or log into important accounts while connected to unsecured campus or cafe Wi-Fi networks.
4. **Download apps only from official stores:** Stick to the Google Play Store or Apple App Store to avoid malware that can steal your credentials.
5. **Keep your software updated:** Ensure your phone's operating system and all apps are running the latest versions to patch security vulnerabilities.
6. **Lock your devices:** Always use a secure biometric or passcode lock on your smartphone and laptop.

### Financial & UPI Safety
7. **Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money:** A PIN is strictly required to *send* money or check your balance, never to receive it.
8. **Verify the receiver's UPI ID:** Always double-check the registered name that pops up on your screen before confirming any transaction.

### Emergency Reporting Channels
If you or someone you know is a victim of cybercrime in India, report it immediately:
* **National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
* **National Cyber Crime Helpline Number:** Call **1930** (Available 24x7)
